Scenario 1: Hospital Management System (HMS)
Weighting and Estimation Phase
------------------------------------------------------------
User Story Backlog with Story Points, Complexity, and Priority

1. As a patient, I want to register my personal and medical information so that my health record is stored in the system.
   Story Points: 3
   Complexity: Low
   Priority: High

2. As a patient, I want to book an appointment with a doctor so that I can get medical consultation.
   Story Points: 5
   Complexity: Medium
   Priority: High

3. As a doctor, I want to view my daily appointment schedule so that I know which patients to see and when.
   Story Points: 3
   Complexity: Low
   Priority: High

4. As a receptionist, I want to assign patients to beds/rooms when they are admitted so that room occupancy is managed.
   Story Points: 5
   Complexity: Medium
   Priority: Medium

5. As a pharmacist, I want to manage the drug inventory so that I know when to reorder medicines.
   Story Points: 5
   Complexity: Medium
   Priority: Medium

6. As a billing clerk, I want to generate invoices for treatments and services so that patients can pay appropriately.
   Story Points: 5
   Complexity: Medium
   Priority: High

7. As an insurance manager, I want to track insurance coverage for bills so that I know the patient’s payment responsibility.
   Story Points: 3
   Complexity: Medium
   Priority: Medium

8. As a hospital admin, I want to generate reports (patients, revenue, departments) so that I can make data-driven decisions.
   Story Points: 8
   Complexity: High
   Priority: High

9. As a patient, I want to receive reminders before appointments so that I don’t miss them.
   Story Points: 3
   Complexity: Low
   Priority: Medium

10. As a doctor, I want to access a patient’s full medical record so I can make informed decisions.
    Story Points: 8
    Complexity: High
    Priority: High

11. As a security officer, I want role-based access control so that only authorized users access sensitive data.
    Story Points: 5
    Complexity: Medium
    Priority: High

12. As a medical coder, I want to use ICD-10 codes for diagnoses so that records are standardized.
    Story Points: 3
    Complexity: Low
    Priority: Medium

------------------------------------------------------------
Notes:
- Story Points: Estimation of effort required to complete the story (e.g., 1-13 Fibonacci scale)
- Complexity: Technical difficulty of implementing the story (Low, Medium, High)
- Priority: Business or user importance (High, Medium, Low)
- These estimations can be refined using Planning Poker, T-Shirt sizing, or other Agile estimation techniques.
